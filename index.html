About Sean and Tomas

Sean Grogan and Tomas Brogan are Afro-Luxembourgish songwriters with Irish ancestry whose lyrical ballads and satirical songs explore exile, identity, devotion, and drink. Their work blends their interpretation of the Irish folk tradition, nodding fondly to the Clancy Brothers and Finbar Furey and Feargal Sharkey, with a contemporary diasporic voice that resounds from the streets of Luxembourg and Africa. That voice that sometimes strays into unknown places, into little known lands, with which they are intimately familiar.


Is amhránaithe Afracacha-Lochsluaineacha iad Sean Grogan agus Tomás Brogan a bhfuil oidhreacht Ghaelach acu, agus a ndéanann a mballáidí líreacacha agus a gcuid amhrán magúla iniúchadh ar dhíbirt, féiniúlacht, tiomantas agus ól. Meascann a gcuid oibre an traidisiún amhránaíochta Gaelach mar a thuigeann siadsan é — le nod dáiríre do na Clancy Brothers agus Finbar Furey agus Feargal Sharkey — le guth comhaimseartha diaspóra a chloistear ó shráideanna Lochsluaine agus na hAfraice. Uaireanta téann an guth sin ar strae, ag dul isteach i gceantair anaithnide, i dtailte beag aithnidiúla atá ar eolas acu féin go domhain.
