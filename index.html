About Sean and Tomas

Sean Grogan and Tomas Brogan are Luxembourg-based songwriters of Irish descent whose lyrical ballads and satirical songs explore exile, identity, devotion, and drink. Their work blends the heart of the Irish folk tradition with a contemporary diasporic voice, that sometimes strays into unknown places, in little known lands, with which they are intimately familiar.
